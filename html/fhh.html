Update My Family Health History

After adding immediate family information, you can:

  1. Check over the relatives in the family member table by doing the following:
    • add health history for each family member by pressing on the symbol in the Add History column
    • update health information for you or anyone by pressing on the symbol in the update history column
    • remove a family member by pressing on the symbol in the remove relative column
    • Add additional relatives by pressing the "Add Another Family Member" button
    • Save your family health history to your computer by clicking on "Save Family History"
  2. On all pages including this page, you can also:
    • Start a new history by pressing on "Home."
    • Return to the screen showing a table of your family by pressing "My Family."
    • Make a copy of your family health history for a family member by pressing "Copy for Family Member."
    • View a diagram of your family tree and a table of your family health history by pressing "View Diagram & Table."
    • Get extra help by pressing "Get Help."
